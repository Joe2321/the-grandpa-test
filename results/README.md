# 📊 Model Responses — Highlight Reel

Each model was given the same story and 6 questions. Below are the most revealing answers — **Q3** (relationship trap), **Q5** (grandpa's intent), and **Q6** (what would you do?) — that best expose the gap between models that "get it" and models that don't.

Full responses are in [`raw/`](raw/).

> **Scoring:** Q3 is the critical trap. "老爺爺" (old grandpa) in Chinese is a polite term for *any* elderly man — it does NOT imply a blood relationship. Models that assume "祖孫關係" (grandchild relationship) have fallen for the same trick as the children in the story.
